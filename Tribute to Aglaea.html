About Lady Aglaea

The Dressmaster, Aglaea, the "Dux Golden Weaver", and the Golden Weaver, holding the Coreflame of Romance, associating Titan Mnestia. The Golden Weaveress who prophets her own end.

Achievements:

  • Architect of the "World Wound Web": She created and maintained a planetary-scale information network known as the World Wound Web.
  • Thousand Leadership of Flame Chase Journey: After Cerydra disappeared unknowingly, she was ascended as the leader of Flame Chase Journey for 1000 years
  • The Interpreter of Prophecy: She interpreted the prophecies of every Chryos Heirs including herself.
