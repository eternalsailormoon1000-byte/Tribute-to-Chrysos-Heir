About Lady Hysilens

The daughter of the sea, Hysilens, the "Dux Gladiorium", and the right man of Imperator Cerydra, holding the Coreflame of Ocean, associating Titan Phagousa. The warrior called "Slayer of Sixty Thousand Foes" earning that because of her cold-blooded warrior on Okhema.

Achievements:

  • Knight Commander of Okhema: Hysilens served as the dedicated, loyal Knight Commander (also known as Dux Gladiorum or Wave-Strumming Knight) under Cerydra.
  • Defeating the Allied Forces: She played a major role in the army that stopped the allied forces of Loukas, Icaria, and Corinth from taking over Okhema.
  • Initiating the Flame-Chase Journey: Following the war, she was instrumental in the start of the first Flame-Chase journey.
  • Fighting the Black Tide: She fought against the corruption of the Black Tide for a thousand years, demonstrating immense willpower and combat prowess.
